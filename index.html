About Me

Hello! My name is Caleb, and I am a mechanical engineer with an expansive technical foundation and global perspective shaped by my life and education abroad. As a University of Toronto Alumni, of the graduating class of 2026, my experience spans mechanical design, prototyping, and automation, complemented by programming skills in Python, C#, and PLC languages, and advanced knowledge and experience with SolidWorks, MATLAB, and custom PCB development. Having traveled to over 30 countries, I have developed adaptability, open-mindedness, and patience; qualities that guide my approach to engineering problems and collaborative design. I am driven by curiosity for how things work, a commitment to creating solutions with tangible results, and a genuine appreciation for teamwork and hands-on learning.
